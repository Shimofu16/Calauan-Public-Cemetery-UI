Calauan Public Cemetery

The Calauan Public Cemetery holds profound significance as the final resting place for the deceased individuals of the Calauan community. This place is thoughtfully divided into two distinct areas. The careful documentation of burial details within the Calauan Public Cemetery is entrusted to logbooks and excel. Notably, in the older section, specific tombs lack essential information regarding the identities of the deceased individuals laid to rest. Unraveling this aspect forms a crucial part of understanding the historical and communal nuances embedded in the cemetery's legacy, which is the focus of our study. The cemetery is experiencing a rapid increase in burial demand, leading to overcrowded gravesites and a shortage of available space.
